Welcome to National Conclave on Technology Development

   Research and Development (R&D) is the key component for holistic growth of any country, especially for a developing country like India. The collaborative efforts of all states of India help for exponential growth of our nation. This region hosts the exotic beauty of natural resources with enchanting opportunities and perspectives simultaneously it carries challenges also. The local indigenous technologies and innovations, creating the scope and laying the foundation for entrepreneurship making the North East to achieve a prominent position in the global platform.

   DST TEC-RGU is trying to develop a common platform for the human resources to exchange their research, innovation and business ideas for the exponential developmental growth of the Northâ€“East region. This center aims to agglomerate a vast talent pool, for promotion of Translational Research which is the key component to solve real-life problems.

   This conclave will be held on the sprawling campus of Rajiv Gandhi University, Arunachal Pradesh, which is also known as the land of rising sun.

Mission of the proposed Conclave


  • To create a common interface for interaction of academia and industry in the country.
  • To create an environment conducive to the development of local indigenous technologies and innovations, creating the scope and laying the foundation for entrepreneurship.
  • To help the country achieve the deliverables of the Sustainable Development Goals.
